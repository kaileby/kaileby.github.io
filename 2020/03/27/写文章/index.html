​ 2019年：坚持、毅力的重要性 观点

​ 2018年：是拥有书还是读书 —>实践的重要性 现象

​ 2017年：选简单的课，还是难得课？只有艰险才能进步，应该不惧艰险 现象

​ 2016年：言传身教，榜样的作用(英语一)、旅游(英语二) 观点

　　2015年：手机时态的聚合(英语一)、城市居民春节花销(英语二) 玩手机不好 现象

　　2014年：孝道或关爱老人(英语一)、城镇化或城乡人口对比(英语二) 观点

　　2013年：选择(英语一)、大学生兼职(英语二) 人应该如何作出选则 现象

　　2012年：乐观(英语一)、工作满意度(英语二) 观点

　　2011年：旅途之”余”(英语一)、国内轿车市场品牌份额(英语二) 现象

　　2010年：文化”火锅” (文化融合)(英语一)、手机入网(英语二) 现象

　　2009年：网络的”近”与”远” 现象

　　2008年：合作(的重要性) 观点

　　2007年：乐观心态(是成功的关键) 观点

　　2006年：偶像崇拜 现象

　　2005年：(年轻人应该)赡养父母 观点

　　2004年：终点又是新的起点 观点

　　2003年：温室里的花朵经不起风雨 观点

　　2002年：美国女孩穿着中国传统服饰(中国与世界的文化交流) 现象

　　2001年：(困难的时候，人人都应该献)爱心 观点

　　2000年：全球商业捕鱼(自然生态平衡遭破坏) 观点

命题规律

　　1、常考话题有四大类：道德伦理类、教育文化类、社会生活类、环保生态类。

　　2、常考话题类别隔年反复考察。

　　3、话题贴近大学生生活或与近年社会热点话题。

　　4、命题不会涉及时事政治或敏感话题

　　5、命题体现和谐社会原则：个人自身的和谐发展、个人与他人或与社会的和谐关系、个人与自然的和谐共处。

写文章遵循：起 承 转 合；

形式简单 + 内容深刻 = 好文章

  • 切题
  • 思路清晰 （起、承、转、合；没有废话）
  • 语言正确、得体 （没有明显的语法错误，写出正确的句子）
  • 形式丰富（善用修辞手法，行文技巧：比喻）
  • 字迹清晰

起：描述内容，引出观点 WHAT

  • 用谚语开头；再As is shown in the picture,描述画面；画面说明了XXX 现象，XXX观点，XXX的重要性
  • 直接开门见山：As is shown in the picture, 描述画面
主题词不会写

用名词表示它 Persistance Patriotism

  • Being + 该词的形容词 = 该词的名词
  • 找不到的形容词：守时的
    • Being Money - oriented:贪财
    • Being Target - oriented: 有明确目标
    • Being Time - oriented:守时
    • Being self-oriented:自私
    • self - confidence: 自信 -说自信的好处
      • or lack of confidence: 不自信的坏处
  • 知道反面的词
    • 奉献精神不会写(dedication)
      • Being-selfish 说自私怎么怎么不好

引出图片(赋) – 描绘图片内容 – 根据图片内容得出的个人观点

S1: 引出图片

  • Have you ever thought about what your phone may bring to you in the long run？the cartoon above poses such a question （以设问的手法引出图片）

    对于富有争议的话题，用莎翁的写法 开赋

  • To use a smart phone,or not to,it is a question ,a question posed in the cartoon .(and a question that calls for a great attention today)

    奥巴马式开头 - 赋

  • If there is anyone who still doubts the value of (xx),who still questions the power of (xx)，the cartoon above is your answer. (适用于)

    专业版开头：谚语开头 -赋

  • According to the famous “butterfly theory”,a mistake today may end up with huge disaster in the future,just like what is described in the cartoon.(作文经常是以一个小现象，小问题，反应大道理)

    文青式开头 赋

  • The biggest distance in the world is not between truth and false,but exits when truth is before you,like the cartoon above,yet you fail to understand.(看起来有点狂，让人不是很舒服)

  • The dark night gives us dark eyes,yet we use them to pursuit(search for) light,a simple picture describe a simple scene,yet we use it to ponder for truth.(好是好，就是有点仙，有点啰嗦)

S2: 描绘图片

力求简单，能描述清楚

case

  • 场景1：守门员怕守不住球，觉得球门太大；射门的人怕射不进，觉得球门太小 - 自信

    • 守门员：the goal keeper 如果不会写 == the man in the door
    • 踢球的人：kicker

    时态:用一般现在时

    the man in the door complains that the door is big,while the man with the ball says that the door is too small.

  • 场景2: 两个残疾人，都只有一条腿，互相搀扶前进 – 合作精神

    • the disabled

  • 场景3：人们独处在互联网的网中

    people from different places are connected by the internet,while spend less time to communicate with others in the real world. / while they alienate each other in the real world.

  • 场景4:老人被子女门当成皮球踢来踢去 – 孝顺

    The old is consider as a 遗弃的absord/useless ball by his children

S3：点明观点

  • It shows that

现象本质也是为了通过事情得出一个观点，再由 I 来论述得出这个观点的好坏、重要性；

  • 某一现象越来越普遍 - popular - 这一现象是好的，需要阐述为什么好、(递进)好在哪（对那些方面有好的影响 ），为了凑字数，甚至还可以说说为了越来越来好，应该做些什么

  • 某一现象不好- 这一现象为什么不好、(递进)哪里不好(对那些方面有不好的影响)、应该怎么挽救

承：阐述得出该观点的原因 WHY

为什么要戒烟、提倡孝顺、团队合作、避免盲目崇拜

抽烟不好：

  • S1：再一次强调观点 or 直接跟据理论、官方数据、伦理、客观事实来为上文的观点站台，使得上文的观点更具说服力

    • In accordance with a widely-accepted theory on the issue of smoking and health revealed by the Health Commitet of UN,the leading institute in medicine ,smoking is the biggest killer to the young.(权威结论)

    • Based on a most-updated statistic report conducted by 专家/权威机构/知名杂志，nearly one-fourth of cancers are caused by smoking(数据)

      Poll 民意调查 ；Survey 调查 ； Conclusion

      对于道德、伦理观点：

      • 蒙太奇手法，段首再次强调该观点的正确性：Respecting the old was,is,and will be a virtue of our tradition

      • Miss Gao 反复强调该伦理：Miss Gao ,a teacher who taught me English,a friend when I was in trouble,a lady who is always nice to her student ; 用多个近义词从多方面丰富观点，体现品德、伦理在多方面具体的重要性；

        TeamWorking ,a spirit that encourages people to fight together,a requirement that young man should meet,a virtual that we value greatly,is calling for more attention

      • 拟人手法：witness 见证

        The history of China has witnessed our great concern of the spirit of teamworking （历史见证了我们对团队合作的重视；通过强调有史以来我们就很重要，来为这一伦理道德的正确性站台）

      对于双面都能说的现象(手机 网络)

      • 欲扬先抑的手法:Of course，there are disaggrement on this issue,saying that smarting phone make us lazy and they may pose dangers to our information security.

  • S2 :联系现实，用逻辑分析，推到这一观点、这一品质、这一现象的正确性、错误性,好坏

    • 现象、观点、品质对实际的好坏

      Reality：

      经济方面：sustainable Development

      环境方面：Environment Protection

      能源方面:Resource recycling

      生态方面：Biological diversity/Balance

      社会人文方面：Culture and heritage tradition、social Progress、Innovation、domestic Security 、physical and mental health、Careers Development、Social harmony

      表达好坏

      • 委婉的手法 + 具体好在哪：

        • I could not love you anymore
        • Smoking could not be any worse
        • Smoking is the last habit that children should have,as it harms their brains(用委婉的方式表达好坏+具体好坏在哪)

      • 比喻的手法 + 具体好在哪：

        • Smoking of children is like opening a Pandora box to their growth,with so many disasters 潘多拉魔盒 亚当的苹果

        • Smoking will bring so many troubles in which harms your health in only a tip of an iceberg 冰山一角 （形容带来的坏处、好处很多）

          Teamworking will bring so many advantages ,in which helping you to solve problems is only a tip of iceberge

          Plastic bags will bring so many harms,in which wasting resources is only a tip of iceberge

        • If we can not win the battle against smoking of teenagers,a future in harmony will be nothing but Utopia,easy to see and hard to reach. 乌托邦 + 对社会有害处

          If we do not work as a team,a success shared by all will be nothing but Utopia.

        • If a person smokes at a young age,it becomes the Water-Loo in his life. 滑铁卢

          If a student is absorbed into playing with his smart phone,it becomes Water-Loo in his life

        • If a young man are addicted to smoking,it will be trapped in shell of his own making 作茧自缚

          If we do not protect our environment carefully ,it will be trapped in shell of our own making

        • The damage that smoking may cause us never a flash in the pan,but long-lasting and far-reaching 类似于冰山一角

          The advantage that a smart phone brings to its users is never a flash in the pan ,but long-lasting and far- reaching

          The damage that smoking may cause is never a tempest in the cup but far-reaching and long-lasting (茶杯中的风暴：小事情)

        • 比喻句配合省略句

          • My legs aches ,and with it my leg （以此引彼，可一在说好坏的某个具体时，快速切换到：在另一方面也是同样的好坏）

            Until these issues are resolved ,a technology of behavior will continue be rejected,and with it the only way to solve our problems

            If a person smokes too much,his lungs will be harmed,and with ithis teeth.

            If we use a smart phone in right way,our time management will be improve, and with it our ways of study.

        • 比喻句式

          • 前否后肯式 比喻 not … any more than

            A collection of facts can not be called science any more than a pile of brick can be called a house . (Not … any more than : 前者不怎么样，就像后者也不能怎么怎么样； 前否后肯)

            Money is not important to the young any more than shampoo is important to monks.

            I can not understand you any more than the day understand the night

            Smoke will not bring anything good ,anymore than taking drugs will bring anything good.

          • 前肯后肯式比喻： … , no less than …

            Time is important to the young ,no less than water is important to fish./ sunshine is important to plant.

  • S3: 举例子，举反例 or 举正面例子 （举名人、国家例子，使观点更加丰满，以权威、影响力大的例子为观点站台）

    观点：吸烟有害健康

    • Steve Jobs,founder of the Apple,died of cancer due to his smoking for many years.
    • In Japan,nearly 2000 people die from cancer caused by smoking,accordng to a medical survey ,recently

  • S4:对例子的反述 - 与 S3 相呼应

    用虚拟语气 （跟现实相反）

    • If they hadn’t smoked so much that much,they wouldn’t have gone to such an early tomb （呼应 日本吸烟死的人多）
    • If they hadn’t taken the environment seriously ,the city wouldn’t have been so cleaning. ( 呼应 S3 城市重视环境很干净的例子)

  • S5: 引出下文(下文是 HOW,引出为了肯定这个观点的好处，去做什么；否定这个观点的坏处，该去做什么.一般为呼吁局)

    • Now it is time to face it,and take thoughts into words,and words into action
    • Immediate actions are in demand now

It is not the tome to ask what the society can do for you,but what you can do for the society.

原因断说面度可以写的情况

S1:让步 （先说反面的观点）

  • of course,they are oppsite views concerning this issue,saying that students should focus on books,what’s more.they are words that it is too young for them to start a business.

S2: 转折 （说出自己的观点）

  • However,its benefits and potentials weigh much more than possible dangers.

S3:论述自己的观点的原因(不需要权威站台了，说明自己的观点在各个方面的好处 or 举例子说明，为什么会提出这样的观点)

  • S4: 点明反面观点的缺点，并提出：该缺点是可以解决的

  • when it comes to its possible dangers,they can be avoid if handled properly.

写作文无转，不然篇幅太长

一般行文： 启（亮明观点） 承与转折的排列组合 合(对承与转所说的东西做个总结)

  • 承接：旨在说明为什么得出该观点 - why；
  • 承接的承接：在为什么的基础上，再进一步具体化的去做 -how;
  • 转折：先前的承接存在 “黑天鹅” 事件，不那么完全正确，所以要通过转折补全说明
  • 对转折的承接：
再承 方法段 – HOW

框架

  • S1: 引出方法 - 指出我们需要付诸行动
  • S2: 具体的建议

S1:

  • Although the way is filled with ups and downs,the tide is unable to return.(优美，但是略显啰嗦)

  • To make xx better ,(直接，简单，词少)

  • Although this is not an easy task,the dominoes already starting to fall。

  • To make xx better is not an easy task,the dominoes already starting to fall

    罗斯福名言

  • It is not the time to ask what can be done for us,but what we can be done for us

    of people, by people.for people (英文介词的简约之美)

  • A better tomorrow is shared by all,yet made by each

  • As Nadern once said, we are called to do great things ,yet everyday we are called to do small,with great love.

  • the only way for the triumph of a misled cause is that good people do nothing.（错误的思想要取得成功就是好的人什么都不做）

S2: 具体方法

  1. 政府呼吁、解决

    • The government should play a positive role to ban/reduce /protect/assist xxx
    • The government is supposed to make more efforts in the campaign for （fight against smoking）
    • It’s the government’s special responsibility to deal/cope with the problem

  2. 个人自觉 （从小事做起、榜样力量）

    • A better tomorrow is should by all,yet made by all
    • Brick by brick ,we can build/solve/conquer the problem/dream by our hands.
    • We can start bu ourselves ,saying no to cigarettes with our family and friends

  3. 法律

    • Functional/Effective laws should be passed to protect/prevent teenagers from smoking
    • It is necessary to apply to legal protection to make sure that teenagers won’t be harmed by cigarettes.

  4. 公众意识

    • The common/public should further understand the importance/urgency/danger/value of xxx ;
    • It is urgent to let the public understand that environment is in danger that more people will take part

  5. 教育

    教育往往起正面作用

    • Schools play a special role to let children and teenagers understand that xxx is the key/is harmful
    • In lessons taught in school.that is advisable for teachers to emphasize the value of xxx

  6. 钱（建立资金机构去解决）

    • It is important to produce more financial supports in researches aimed to reduce the 好/坏 of xxx
    • Financial awards should be gain to researchers who can successfully control the 好/坏 of xxx

  7. 提倡品质、道德

    • to have more self-confidence,it is necessary to take little step in daily life,trying to understand your own potential
    • self-confidence doesn’t come naturally ,but originates from what you do every day,so set you goal and 培养 mind and strategies ,can make it each day.
    • It is helpful to set your own example,people who succeeded because of teamwork and try to model their mind and strategies
    • the power of positive example is so huge that learning from them can be very helpful to gain

    一个引出，两个解决方案，即可作为 方法段；感觉方法段很长会超出字数
